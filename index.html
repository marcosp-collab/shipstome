How it works

Everything you need to know
before clicking

ShipsToMe works silently in the background as you browse, giving you all the info upfront.

📦

Shipping detection

Automatically checks every product and marks those that cannot be delivered to your location with a clear red badge.

🔀

Variant awareness

Detects products with multiple variants. If some colors or sizes don't ship to you, it tells you exactly how many.

💰

Price & delivery

Shows current price and estimated delivery date directly on each listing card — without opening the product page.

📈

Price history

Displays a Keepa price chart on every product page so you can see historical prices at a glance before buying.

🎨

Swatch marking

On product pages, individual color and size swatches that don't ship to you are marked with a clear badge.

🔒

100% private

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is collected, stored or shared. Ever.

Visual system

Three states,

A clear visual language tells you instantly what you can and can't order — without entering a single product page.

Optional filters

Filter beyond shipping

Once you've filtered undeliverable products, go further with extra controls.

🏪

Amazon seller only

Hide products sold by third-party sellers and only see items fulfilled directly by Amazon.

⭐

Minimum rating

Filter by star rating — 3★, 3.5★, 4★ or 4.5★ — to avoid poorly reviewed products.

📈

Keepa price chart

Toggle the Keepa price history chart on product pages to track whether a deal is genuinely good.

🔄

Rescan on demand

Manually trigger a rescan of the current page at any time from the extension popup.
